About The Muse Salon

Designed for considered style and everyday confidence.

The Muse Salon is a hair and beauty salon in Prima Setapak for clients who want a refined result, honest advice and a calm appointment from start to finish.

Our Story

A neighbourhood salon with a premium eye.

The Muse Salon serves clients across Setapak and Kuala Lumpur with practical, polished hair services: cuts that sit well, colour that feels wearable, treatments that restore shine and styling that makes the final look feel complete.

  • Consultations that consider face shape, lifestyle and maintenance.
  • Colour and chemical services planned around hair condition.
  • A friendly salon rhythm that feels professional without feeling cold.

Philosophy

Style, confidence, care, consultation.

Hair is personal. We believe the best salon result balances the look you want with the condition, texture and routine you actually have.

01

Style

Modern shapes, clean finishing and colour choices that feel elevated, not overdone.

02

Confidence

The goal is hair that helps you feel composed when you leave and when you style it later.

03

Care

Condition matters, especially for colour, rebonding, perm and treatment work.

04

Consultation

We keep expectations clear before any major transformation or chemical service.

What To Expect

A service flow that feels easy.

  • A short conversation about your current hair, concerns and desired look.
  • Service recommendations based on your hair history and upkeep preferences.
  • A polished blow dry or finish so you can see the shape and texture clearly.
  • Simple aftercare advice where it matters, especially after colour or treatment.
